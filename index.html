The Problem

People love keeping indoor plants but struggle with plant care because:

  • They often forget to water their plants regularly
  • They don't understand specific sunlight and care requirements
  • Plants die by accident due to lack of knowledge or time
  • No guidance on plant health issues or diseases
  • Difficulty finding the right supplies and tools
  • Overwhelming amount of conflicting plant care information online

Our Solution

PlantBuddy is a mobile and web app that revolutionizes plant care by offering:

  1. Plant Scanner: Scan your plant with camera to instantly identify species and care needs
  2. Real-time Care Instructions: Get personalized watering, sunlight, and fertilizing schedules
  3. Smart Reminders: Never forget to water or care for your plants again
  4. AI Health Diagnosis: Use AI to identify plant diseases and health issues
  5. Online Plant Shop: Order fertilizers, pots, tools, and supplies directly through the app
  6. Plant Library: Access comprehensive database of plant care information
  7. Expert Chat: Connect with certified horticulturists for professional advice

Target Market

Our primary customers include:

  • Urban Dwellers: City residents with indoor plants seeking easy care solutions
  • Busy Professionals: Working individuals who want plants but lack time for proper care
  • Gen Z and Millennials: Young adults who care about home aesthetics and plant trends
  • Eco-conscious Individuals: People passionate about sustainable living and green spaces
  • New Plant Parents: First-time plant owners needing guidance and support

Market Size: $2.7 billion global houseplant market, growing at 9.2% annually
